Privacy

Introduction

Welcome to the DailyStyle Privacy Policy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your information on our website, which serves as both a landing page and a blog. It explains your rights and choices regarding your personal data. By accessing or using DailyStyle, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.
Information We Collect
DailyStyle collects various types of personal information to enhance your experience. This includes data you provide directly, such as your name and email address, as well as information collected automatically, such as IP addresses and cookies. We do not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is utilized for various purposes: to provide our services, improve user experience, and communicate with you about updates or offers. For users who subscribe to our blog, we use your email for notifications regarding new content.

Sharing Your Information
Your information is shared solely for essential functions. We do not sell your personal data. However, we use third-party services for payment processing, which may independently collect and handle your data as necessary for these transactions.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and analyze site traffic. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings, but disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our site.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy. After this period, your data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

Your Rights

Under U.S. privacy laws, you have the right to access, correct, delete, or object to the processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at admin@DailyStyle.com.

Security Measures

DailyStyle employs robust security protocols to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Policy Updates

We may periodically update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Significant updates will be communicated to you via email or a notice on our website. The effective date of the latest version will always be posted at the top of this policy.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at admin@DailyStyle.com.

DailyStyle
DailyStyle was born in 2012 from a deep appreciation for the art of knitwear and a personal love for the warmth of winter sweaters. Our founder, an avid collector of vintage sweaters and textile enthusiast, recognized a gap in the market for expertly curated knitwear resources and styles that blend tradition with modern sensibilities. Determined to share this passion, DailyStyle evolved from a small blog into a renowned collective of experts dedicated to delivering quality insights and products in the realm of winter fashion.
